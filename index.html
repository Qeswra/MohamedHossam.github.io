Air Lines Loyalty Program Analysis

As a Data Analyst, I had the privilege of working on an in-depth analysis of airline loyalty programs. Our project revealed that these programs can increase customer retention by up to 30% and boost revenue by 20%. We analyzed how airlines leverage data for dynamic pricing, resulting in a 15% increase in seat occupancy. Additionally, we explored the extensive partner ecosystems, which contribute to 25% of the total program value. We utilized several tools to achieve these insights: SQL: For storing and querying large datasets efficiently. Python: For data cleaning, transformation, and advanced analytics. Power Query: For data extraction and transformation. Power BI: For creating interactive dashboards and visualizations. Tableau: For advanced data visualization and storytelling.

Bikes Store Sales Analysis

• I’m excited to share a project our team just wrapped up—a full-lifecycle sales analysis for our bike stores, built end-to-end with SQL, Power Pivot, DAX and interactive dashboards. Here’s what went down: • ETL & Data Modeling: – Extracted and cleaned raw tables from our SQL warehouse – Designed a star schema around a central “Sales Order Item” fact table – Engineered DAX measures for Total Sales, Cost, Profit, AOV, Quantity and more • KPI-Driven Dashboards: – Home page summary with real-time totals ($7.7 M sales, $1.85 M profit, 7 078 units sold) – Orders Dashboard tracking trends by month, state and status – Customer Dashboard showcasing RFM segments and loyalty insights • Key Findings: – Sales spike May–July (July 2017 saw +18% YoY growth) – Mountain & Electric bikes represent ~50% of revenue – Top-performing store drives nearly half of all sales – High-value customers generate 60% of repeat orders • Recommendations: – Short-term: bolster Electric & Mountain bike inventory; localized promotions for underperformers – Mid-term: loyalty programs and peak-season staffing optimizations – Long-term: dynamic pricing pilots on best-selling SKUs and expansion in TX/CA • Projected Impact: – 8–12% uplift in peak-season revenue – 3–5% margin improvement – Payback in 4–6 months
